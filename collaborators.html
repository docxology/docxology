Principal Collaborators

KF
Karl Friston
UCL, London
Active Inference, Free Energy Principle

Originator of the free energy principle. Collaboration began with "Of woodlice and men" (2018) and continues through co-authored papers on variational synthesis, evolutionary dynamics, and federated inference.

DG
Deborah Gordon
Stanford University
Entomology, Collective Behavior

PhD advisor. Foundational work on the behavioral, physiological, and transcriptomic bases of collective behavior in harvester ants.

UC Davis

Co-mentored NSF Postdoctoral Fellowship at UC Davis (2020–2023), focusing on distributed physiology and evolutionary genomics in eusocial insects.

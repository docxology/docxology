## Abstract

AGEINT (Agentic Intelligence) is a local curriculum-and-assurance atlas for teaching bounded AI-agent support inside intelligence education by making the machinery of Synthetic Analytic Tradecraft visible on the page. It converts a Structured-Intelligence-Studies guide's table of contents and bibliography into 16 parts, 51 modules, 9 methods appendices, 20 named AGEINT patterns, and 312 parsed source references, framed around cognitive security, structured analytic techniques, and Active Inference.

## Abstract

The primary objectives of this Push and Pull document are to explore the decoupling of cognitive ( covert , attentional) and bodily ( overt ) behaviors , and to provide a sequence of hands-on computer exercises for further exploration . The priming sequence of exercises is presented to inform an individual&rsquo;s understanding of how their attention interacts with computer use movements, such as controlling the on-screen cursor with mouse, touchpad, or eye movements. What is presented here is only an initial sequence to complement other and future development of &ldquo;hand-I&rdquo; uncouplings, in principle and in practice.

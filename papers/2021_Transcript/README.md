## Abstract

On June 21st, 2021, Active Inference Lab ( activeinference.org/ ) hosted its first Applied Active Inference Symposium, featuring Professor Karl Friston. The Symposium was structured in three sections, corresponding to the Organizational Units of the Active Inference Lab: Education, Communication, and Tools. This publication reflects an edited and enriched transcript of the proceedings of the Symposium.

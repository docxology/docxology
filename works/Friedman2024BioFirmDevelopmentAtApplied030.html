Overview

Extracted from the local paper documentation when available.

BioFirm applies Active Inference and the Free Energy Principle to organizational and bioregional stewardship. The framework models organizations as biological-inspired adaptive systems, using active inference to optimize resource management and collective decision-making in bioregional governance contexts.

Overview

Extracted from the local paper documentation when available.

This paper explores the juxtaposition of the lives and ideas of William Blake and Buckminster Fuller, focusing on the concept of 'Comprehensivity' as a means to foster a deeper understanding of the world. It invites collaboration and engagement in developing multi-perspectival insights through inquiry and action.
